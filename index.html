ğŸ“– Lesson 1: Understanding Money

What is Money? Money is what we use to buy things we need and want. We earn money by working, and we can use it to buy food, clothes, toys, or save it for later.

Key Idea: Money is limited, so we must choose how to use it wisely!

ğŸ’° Lesson 2: Needs vs. Wants

Needs are things you must have to live: food, water, shelter, clothes.

Wants are things that are nice to have but not necessary: toys, candy, new games.

Smart Tip: Always pay for needs first, then wants if you have money left!
